Who We Are

India's Modern Footwear Destination

STRIDE is a proudly Indian footwear platform that celebrates the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship while embracing modern design aesthetics. We bring you the finest footwear — from handcrafted Rajasthani Juttis and authentic Kolhapuri Chappals to cutting-edge running shoes and fashion-forward sneakers.

Founded with the belief that every step should tell a story, STRIDE bridges the gap between tradition and modernity. Whether you're preparing for a festive celebration, a corporate meeting, or a weekend trek in the Sahyadris — we've got the perfect pair for you.

Our Mission

To deliver stylish, comfortable, and affordable footwear to every Indian household — honouring our rich cultural heritage while keeping pace with global fashion trends. We believe every Indian deserves world-class footwear at fair prices.

  • ✦ Affordable premium quality
  • ✦ Support Indian artisans
  • ✦ Sustainable practices
🔭

Our Vision

To become India's leading digital footwear destination — a platform where tradition and technology unite to create an unparalleled shopping experience. By 2030, we aim to serve customers across all 28 states and 8 union territories.

  • ✦ Pan-India presence
  • ✦ 10,000+ artisan partnerships
  • ✦ Zero-waste manufacturing

Cultural Heritage

Indian Inspiration 🇮🇳

India has a 5,000-year legacy of footwear craftsmanship. We're bringing these timeless traditions to the digital age.

👞

Jutti / Mojari

Punjab & Rajasthan

Handcrafted embroidered shoes with intricate mirror work, phulkari patterns, and traditional motifs. Worn for centuries across North India.

🩴

Kolhapuri Chappal

Maharashtra

Iconic hand-stitched leather sandals from Kolhapur. Made using vegetable-tanned buffalo leather with distinctive punching patterns.

👑

Nagra Shoes

North India

Curved-toe formal footwear with gold or silver thread embroidery. Traditionally paired with sherwanis and Jodhpuri suits.

✨

Indo-Western Fusion

Modern India

Contemporary designs featuring traditional embroidery (zardozi, thread work) on modern silhouettes — block heels, platforms, and flats.

🪷

Supporting Indian Artisans

Every traditional footwear purchase directly supports skilled artisans from Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Punjab, and other states. We partner with over 200 artisan families to preserve India's footwear heritage.
